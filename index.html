WHAT IS BUILD GUILD?

A 1-day hardware meetup organized as part of Hack Club Blueprint's global Build Guilds week. Hack Club funds local organizers to run events in their cities. Free, open to any teen who's curious about circuits, boards, and hardware.

IS IT FREE?

Yes! 100% free. Hack Club covers all costs including materials, tools, food, and venue. No hidden fees.

DO I NEED HARDWARE EXPERIENCE?

Absolutely not! This event is designed for complete beginners. We'll teach you everything. Just bring curiosity and enthusiasm.

WHO CAN ATTEND?

Anyone aged 13-18 can attend, including someone who is 18 years and 5 months old. Beginners and experienced builders are all welcome.

WHAT SHOULD I BRING?

Just yourself! Bring a laptop if you have one, but we have spares. All tools and components are provided.

WHERE IS THE VENUE?

Location to be announced soon. We're working on securing a central, accessible venue in Bhopal.

WILL THERE BE FOOD?

Yes! Lunch and snacks are included throughout the day. Let us know about dietary restrictions during registration.

READY TO JOIN THE GUILD?

Come for a fun, friendly teen meetup where you can try hardware, make friends, and build cool stuff even if you're starting from zero.

PART OF A GLOBAL MOVEMENT

Build Guild is part of Hack Club Blueprint's Global Build Guilds Week (April 13-19, 2026). This week, 100+ cities around the world are running hardware events for teens. From New York to Tokyo, from São Paulo to Singapore — we're building together.

WHAT IS HACK CLUB BLUEPRINT?

Blueprint is Hack Club's global hardware initiative. We empower teenagers to learn electronics, design PCBs, build drones, and ship real hardware projects. Founded by teens, funded by Hack Club (a 501(c)(3) nonprofit), and run for teens around the world.

50,000+ teen makers in the Hack Club community. 350+ clubs running in schools and communities worldwide. Completely free.
